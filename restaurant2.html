Hotel Mandalay

Surrounded by restaurants and cafes, this straightforward hotel lies 2 km from Mahamuni Buddha Temple, a pilgrimage site, and 4 km from the elegant Mandalay Palace. The warmly decorated rooms feature wood accents, and provide free Wi-Fi, cable TV and minibars. Suites add whirlpool baths and sitting areas. Room service is available. Parking and a breakfast buffet are complimentary. There’s an airy cafe and an informal restaurant. Other amenities include a gym, a garden and an outdoor pool.

Address

No.(562), 78 street, Between 37 street and 38 street, Haymamarlar Taung Ward, Mahaaungmyae Township, Mandalay

Hotel Marvel

This casual hotel set above Mandalay train station is 4 km from Mahamuni Buddha Temple and 3 km from Mandalay Palace. The airy rooms adorned in blue feature flat-screen TVs, free Wi-Fi, and tea and coffeemaking facilities, plus tiled wet rooms. There are 2 relaxed restaurants, as well as a rooftop bar and a lavishly decorated karaoke room. Other amenities include a spa with treatment rooms, and nearby secured parking.

Address

78st x 30st, Mandalay
