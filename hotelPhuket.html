Stay Wellbeing & Lifestyle Resort

Relax at a lifestyle resort which offers an oasis of space and comforting environment. Welcome to STAY-PHUKET, a spacious contemporary and luxurious wellbeing resort overlooking Rawai Bay. We are located in a private and beautifully quiet area of Rawai, south of Phuket. STAY resort offers modern and chic junior suites, one bedroom suites, 2 bedroom suites and 3 bedroom villas with a private pool and main pool access. Enjoy a stress-free fitness holiday. Exercise at Stayfit, our fully equipped gym, and take part in HIIT (High Intensity Interval Training) classes and Yoga under expert supervision. Understanding the importance of being eco-friendly, we developed our STAY Green initiative to ensure we offer with minimal impact and maximum sustainability for our treasured environment.

Address

Address-Soi Suksan 2, Rawai, Mueang Phuket District, Chang Wat Phuket 83130, Thai

Banyan Tree Phuket

Escape to a Sanctuary for the Senses at the award winning Banyan Tree Phuket located in the Andaman’s greatest treasure, the island of Phuket. A private paradise of luxurious all-pool villas nestled around a salt water lagoon and surrounded by lush greenery. This is the perfect destination for travellers who seek a luxury hideaway, a Golf Getaway, a relaxing staycation or a base from which to explore this beautiful island its heritage, culture and stunning beaches. Step into a world of unspoilt natural beauty and unparalleled luxury at Banyan Tree Phuket.

Address

33, 33/27 Moo 4, Srisoonthorn Road, Cherngtalay, Amphur Talang, Phuket 83110, Thailand

JW Marriott Phuket Hotel

Marriott has all the bases covered for an active holiday, regardless of weather conditions.Just how Marriott has managed to keep the personal touch in such large grounds is a mystery, yet also a welcome surprise.The Marriott is directly adjacent to Mai Khao Beach in northwest Phuket next to the Sirinath Marine National Park and protected sea turtle area.Some 15 minutes north of Phuket International Airport, it also has easy access to neighbouring Phang Nga Province.The well-appointed rooms are spacious with a minimum area of 47sqm and each room features a balcony and an indoor sala - a Thai-style raised area.

Address

Address-18 Soi Hat Surin 8, Choeng Thale, Thalang District, Phuket 83110,Thai

Amari Phuket Hotel Hotel

Amari Phuket, a 4.5 star hotel located just to the south of Patong, is a 345-room and suite hotel where each accommodation choice faces the Andaman Sea and Patong Bay.Views and vistas across the bay are spectacular and set the tone for this 4.5 star multi-tiered hillside hotel.Amari is set in the hills and effectively straddles a slope heading down to the sea.It has two swimming pools, a scuba dive centre, a spa, tailor shop and a fitness centre as well as several quality restaurants with a reputation for well thought out dishes and themed evenings

Address

168 Moo 6, Layan Beach Soi 4 Cherngtalay Phuket Thailand
